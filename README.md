## üìà Key Analytical Features

- **Comprehensive Transaction Overview**: Instantly view the total number of transactions, aggregate amounts, and key statistics (sum, maximum, minimum) for rapid assessment of financial activity.
- **Balance Evolution by Account**: Analyze trends in account balances over time, segmented by account number, to monitor growth, stability, or anomalies.
- **Transaction Type Analysis**: Break down transaction amounts by year and type (Credit/Debit) for a clear understanding of inflows and outflows.
- **Category-Based Spending Insights**: Identify spending patterns across categories such as Salary, Food, Shopping, Utilities, and Entertainment, supporting targeted cost optimization.
- **Dynamic, Multi-Dimensional Filtering**: Refine your analysis with intuitive filters for date range, category, transaction type, and account number, enabling granular exploration of your data.
- **Professional Visualizations**: Benefit from a modern, visually appealing interface with high-contrast charts, summary cards, and a sophisticated background for enhanced readability and engagement.
